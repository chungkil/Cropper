Good to know

Frequently asked

Do I need any Korean to start?

None at all. The app is built for absolute beginners — you’ll learn to read Hangul from the very first letter, with mnemonics and audio for every character.

Does it work offline?

Yes. All learning content — 975 words, 30 dialogues, quizzes, flashcards, handwriting and pronunciation rules — works fully offline. Only ads and subscription checks need a connection.

What languages does the app support?

The interface and all translations are available in English, Japanese and Chinese (Simplified). The app follows your device language automatically.

How does the Smart Quiz work?

It analyzes your quiz history to spot the words you struggle with, then prioritizes those in future quizzes — so you spend your time on exactly what you need.

Is it really free to start?

Yes. The free tier includes all 40 letters, 400+ words, handwriting, daily quizzes and flashcards. Premium unlocks the full 975-word library, all dialogues, advanced practice and an ad-free experience.
