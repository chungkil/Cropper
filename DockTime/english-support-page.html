Simple Dock Tile Clock

App Overview

Simple Dock Tile Clock is a lightweight utility that displays real-time clock on your macOS Dock. Choose between digital or analog clock options with various formats and styles to personalize your experience.

Time Format Options

Select from Short (12:34), Medium (12:34:56), Full (12:34:56 PM), or Analog clock

Customizable Design

Adjust font size and text color to match your preferences

Analog Clock Options

Customize hour/minute hand color, toggle second hand, and select second hand color

Preset Management

Save your favorite settings and quickly switch between them

System Requirements

  • macOS 14.0 or later
  • Compatible with both Apple Silicon and Intel Mac

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does Simple Dock Tile Clock use significant system resources?

A: No. The app is designed to be very lightweight and uses minimal system resources.

Q: How do I change the language settings?

A: Simple Dock Tile Clock automatically follows your system language settings. Currently, English and Korean are supported.

Q: Can I make it run automatically at login?

A: Yes. Add Simple Dock Tile Clock to your login items in System Settings > Users & Groups > Login Items.

Q: Can I share presets with another Mac?

A: Yes, use the 'Export' feature in the preset management section to save your preset as a .json file that can be shared and imported on another Mac.

Privacy Policy

Simple Dock Tile Clock does not collect any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device only. Exported preset files do not contain any personally identifiable information.
