Privacy Policy - Simple Dock Tile Clock

Last Updated: March 3, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Simple Dock Tile Clock. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding any information collected through our application.

Information We Collect

Simple Dock Tile Clock does not collect or transmit any personal information. The application operates entirely on your local device with the following limited data usage:

  • User Preferences: Settings such as time format, font size, colors, and presets are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults.
  • Security-Scoped Bookmarks: When exporting presets to files, the application creates security-scoped bookmarks to maintain file access permissions as required by macOS Sandbox security model.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Simple Dock Tile Clock does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertisement networks.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data from you.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. Your preferences data is entirely under your control and can be reset by clearing the application's preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
