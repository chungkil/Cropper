Roguelike Brick Breaker Action

Classic brick breaking meets roguelike progression! Choose power-ups after each stage and conquer all 20 stages. No two runs are ever the same.

  • 8 unique upgrades to choose from
  • Multi-Ball, Piercing Shot, Explosive Ball
  • Build your perfect combo each run

🧱 Diverse Bricks

  • Normal - One hit destroy
  • Reinforced - Takes 3 hits
  • Explosive - Area damage
  • Shooter - Fires projectiles
  • Support - Chain collapse

🎯 Strategic Gameplay

  • 20 stages + Boss battles
  • 3 enemy attack patterns
  • Combo system for high scores
  • Special stage patterns

🎮 Simple Controls

  • Drag to move paddle
  • Tap to launch ball
  • Easy to learn
  • Hard to master
