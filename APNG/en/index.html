Live Photo Converter

Turn Live Photos into APNG, GIF, WebP, or video

About

Live Photo Converter is the simplest way to turn your iPhone Live Photos into APNG, GIF, WebP, or video (mp4). Pick a Live Photo from your library, choose a format, and the app converts it automatically. You can preview the result in the app, share it, or save it to Photos and Files.

Features

Four output formats

APNG (high quality, transparent), GIF (universal), WebP (efficient), and video (mp4, with sound).

Keeps the sound

Export to video (mp4) to preserve the audio from your Live Photo.

In-app preview

Play back the converted result right inside the app.

File management

Revisit, share, and delete your conversions from My Files.

How to use

  1. Launch the app and tap Select Live Photo.
  2. Pick a Live Photo from your library.
  3. Choose APNG, GIF, WebP, or video as the output format.
  4. Preview the result, then share it or revisit it from My Files.
